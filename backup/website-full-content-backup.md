## Page: Automation
**URL:** https://www.newlens.uk/services/automation

### Automate the admin. Keep the human work human.
newlens designs and implements practical automations that remove copying, chasing, retyping, and manual updates.

#### Common problems we fix
- Enquiries get missed or handled inconsistently.
- Quotes, bookings, and follow-ups take too long.
- Invoices and chasing are manual and messy.
- Data ends up duplicated across spreadsheets, email, and CRM.
- Reporting takes hours every week.

#### Typical automation examples
- Enquiry capture and triage from email or forms.
- Auto-replies that ask the right questions and route work to the right person.
- Quote and booking workflows.
- Invoice reminders and status tracking.
- CRM updates and clean handovers between systems.
- Internal reporting refreshes and weekly summaries.

#### How delivery works
- **Discovery and mapping:** We pick one process and map the current steps, systems, and risks.
- **Build and configure:** We implement the workflow, integrations, and logging.
- **Test and launch:** We test edge cases, confirm success measures, then go live.
- **Monitor and improve:** Light-touch support to keep things stable and adjust as you learn.
